About Me

First thing I would want anyone to know about me is that I am a believer, follower, and lover of Jesus Christ. My life has forever been changed by him and I thank him for everything. As for the person who wrote this, Chris is a proud native of Decatur,GA. Happily married and a father as of the end of 2023. He loves his family and believes his skills exist to make their lives and people around them better.

As for background, there's several stops we have to make. Originally studied to be an engineer at Georgia Tech and graduated with a degree in mechanical engineering. Worked as an engineer for a while then took a complete career shift to follow a passion earlier than expected. Decided to jump into education for 4 years at my old high school teaching math, coaching softball, mentoring, and developing some truly amazing relationships with students and colleauges. Once we had our first child, it was time to make a move that let me pour the same energy to him as I did my students. So I jumped back into the STEM world.

Now I am at a place whatever I do, I want it to present a reasonable challenge while still providing for my family. I've realized that my skills allow me to be solution-oriented and creative but now I want to channel those parts of me into something that I can see myself doing everyday. I find myself in the IT/Web Dev space and look forward to each new opportunity that comes my way.
